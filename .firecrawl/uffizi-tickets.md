## Ticket info and purchasing methods

Buy online

On the website [**tickets.uffizi.it**](https://tickets.uffizi.it/ "https://tickets.uffizi.it/")

Buy via Call Center

**800 615 615** \- Toll-free number **+39 055 0354135**\- From abroad, mobile phones, public offices not authorised to use the toll-free number (e.g. schools)

Monday to Sunday, from 8:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m.

Buy at the ticket office

The ticket offices are located under the loggia of the Uffizi, in the square in front of Pitti Palace, and at the entrances to the Boboli Gardens (Annalena in Via Romana, Porta Romana, Forte Belvedere).

### Combined ticket for all museums

Combined ticket, valid for 5 consecutive days, offering priority admission to all the monumental complexes of the Uffizi, Pitti Palace and Boboli Gardens.

The validity extends to the sixth day only if the 5 consecutive days include Monday (when the museums are ordinarily closed), 25th December and 1st January.

[Read all](https://www.uffizi.it/en/pages/combined-ticket "Combined ticket Go to (external link)")

### Passepartout. Annual Subscriptions

It is possible to visit the Museums of the Uffizi Galleries by purchasing an annual subscription, which includes all temporary exhibitions and priority admission.

### Extra fee for groups on the Uffizi ticket

Groups of 11 people or more are required to pay an extra charge of 70 € to be admitted to the Uffizi.

[Read all](https://www.uffizi.it/en/notices/extra-fee-groups "Extra fee groups Go to (external link)")

### Firenze Card

Special cumulative ticket for admission to the museums of the metropolitan area of Florence.

During the days (or time slots) of free admission to the museum, priority admission is suspended except for disabled visitors and people accompanying them by law, as well as pregnant women.

Reservation for the Uffizi are made through the call center (number: 800 615615; from abroad: +39 055 0354135 – available Monday to Sunday, from 8:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m.), at the physical ticket offices or online at [**tickets.uffizi.it**](https://tickets.uffizi.it/ "https://tickets.uffizi.it/")(once you have selected your preferred day and time, choose the option “Uffizi reservation for Firenze Card owners”).

[Read all](https://www.firenzecard.it/en?lang=en "Firenze Card Go to (external link)")
