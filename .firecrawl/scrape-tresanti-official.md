## A place to be

Push open the doors of Concettina ai Tre Santi and the magic happens every time: you leave behind the chaos in the streets and their hordes of motorbikes for the slow, radiant rhythms of the pizzeria’s universe. Here you are immediately enfolded in a spirited, intimate atmosphere, both in the dining rooms and in the garden patio. It’s so easy to tune yourself to the house frequency…

![](https://www.concettinaaitresanti.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/RROB2296_L2-1024x615.jpg)

#### Naples and the Sanità quarter permeate the pizzeria with their colours and fragrances

![](https://www.concettinaaitresanti.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/tavolo-preparazione-luci-concettina-650x650.jpg)

![](https://www.concettinaaitresanti.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/posate-piatti-concettina.jpg)

Look around: everything, the impressive Pulcinella signed art piece, the majolica dishes, all the cooking and service-ware created by artists of the Sanità exhibit the Olivas’ lively interest in “all things beautiful.” Generously sharing all that with others is in the very DNA of Naples.

#### Capri: an island between fairy-tale and ancient culture

Capri: Itay’s iconic island showcase of joie de vivre. Now its fabled shores are witnessing the arrival of the Olivas, their bags filled with family tales and the fragrant flavours of four generations of pizzas and their ingredients.

Firewood, white-wash, multi-coloured majolica, and scrumptious taste abound. Designed to make you feel totally at home, Oliva-style: straightforward, casual, and everything fresh.
