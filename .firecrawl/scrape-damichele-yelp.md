From the Pizze section, we went with a \* Marinara. The MARINARA/6€ was pomodoro, origano, aglio, olio, which was tomato, oregano, garlic, oil.



The \* Marinara came out super fast. It was large in my opinion. I fell in love with it at first bite. I believe the taste of the pomodoro will live on in my memory for quite some time. It was plenty for both of us.



The service was fast and friendly. I didn't have any issues.



Overall, this place was an excellent stop with a price that was cheap. I wouldn't hesitate to go again in the future.
